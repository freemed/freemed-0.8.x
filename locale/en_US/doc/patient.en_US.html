The main patient selection screen allows you to select a patient based on a wide range of criteria. The "Show All Patients" link is provided only when the number of patients in the system is very small, as large numbers of patients produce prohibitively large pages to scroll through.

    Patient Selection Methods
  • Patients by Name - Displays all patients whose last name start with the specified letter. This works well in smaller practices, but may become a problem in larger settings, where its use is strongly discouraged.
  • Patients Field Search - Allows searching on a patient's vital information (name, address, et cetera). It currently searches on a substring for any of the pieces of patient information listed in its dropbox.
  • Soundalike Search - Searches via a soundex key any of the fields listed in its dropbox.
From here, you can choose a patient to work with. If only one patient is found who meets your search criteria, you will be automatically brought to their management screen.
